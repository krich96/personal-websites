PROJECT 1

Write a Literature Review on Attention and Multitasking in the Digital Age

Project Overview

Delivered a 60+ page literature review that explored the boundaries of human attention and multitasking. The author used this work to inform the writing of his book, Net Smart: How to Thrive Online

Methods

  • Reviewed the most up-to-date cognitive science research using university libraries
  • Reviewed a variety of opinion and popular science columns selected by the author
  • Synthesized sources and drafted the document
